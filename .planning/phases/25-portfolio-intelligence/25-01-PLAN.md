Props: `contracts: Contract[]`

Computation (useMemo):

3. For each reviewed contract, collect unique categories (Set of categories from findings)

5. Filter to categories appearing in 3+ contracts
6. Sort by count descending

Display: Card styled like StatCard but with a list layout. Title: "Common Patterns" with a Lucide `TrendingUp` or `BarChart3` icon. Show top patterns as "Category Name - X/N contracts" format. If no categories meet the 3+ threshold, hide card entirely.
