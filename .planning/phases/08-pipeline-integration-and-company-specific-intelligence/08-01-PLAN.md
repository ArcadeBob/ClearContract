   - Each factor starts at 100, subtracts severity penalties (Critical: 25, High: 15, Medium: 8, Low: 3, Info: 0) for matching findings
   - Factor-to-finding matching: Bonding matches `sourcePass` containing 'retainage' OR title/description containing 'bond'; Insurance matches `category === 'Insurance Requirements'`; Scope matches `category === 'Scope of Work'`; Payment matches `sourcePass === 'legal-payment-contingency'`; Retainage matches `sourcePass === 'legal-retainage'`
   - Weighted average across factors = final score (0-100)
   - Thresholds: >= 70 = 'bid' / "Bid Recommended", >= 40 = 'caution' / "Proceed with Caution", < 40 = 'no-bid' / "Significant Concerns"
   - Export: `computeBidSignal(findings: Finding[]): BidSignal`
   - Clamp each factor score to 0-100
