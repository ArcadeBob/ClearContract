    schema: IndemnificationFindingSchema,
    convert: (f, pn) => convertIndemnificationFinding(f as IndemnificationFinding, pn),

**Step 5: Update mergePassResults** to use the dispatch map:

In the loop where isLegal or isScope, replace the current cast-heavy approach:
