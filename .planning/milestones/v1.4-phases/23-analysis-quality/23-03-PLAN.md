     2. **Risk Transfer Stack**: Broad indemnification + insurance gaps + flow-down provisions = sub absorbs disproportionate liability

   The synthesis findings are appended AFTER mergePassResults (so they skip dedup -- they are new findings, not duplicates) and AFTER risk score computation (which happens inside mergePassResults). They get IDs in the existing `.map()` step and flow to the client.

   Note on scoring exclusion: Plan 02 already set CATEGORY_WEIGHTS['Compound Risk'] = 0 in scoring.ts. But since mergePassResults computes the risk score BEFORE synthesis findings are appended, synthesis findings never even enter the scoring function. This is double-safe by design.

   - No downgradedFrom (so risk-overview fallback fails)
   So synthesis findings contribute 0 to bid signal. No changes needed in bidSignal.ts.
