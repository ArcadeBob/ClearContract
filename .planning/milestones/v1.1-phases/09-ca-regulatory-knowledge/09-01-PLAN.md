**ca-lien-law.ts** (export name: `caLienLaw`):
- id: 'ca-lien-law', domain: 'regulatory', title: 'California Mechanics Lien Law and Void-by-Law Clauses'
- effectiveDate: '2024-01-01', reviewByDate: '2027-01-01'
- Content must include ALL of the following (structured as Claude analysis instructions, not legal reference):
  - Preliminary notice: 20-day requirement for subs/suppliers from first furnishing (CC 8200-8216), late notice only covers work within 20 days before notice sent
  - Recording deadlines: WITHOUT Notice of Completion = 90 days from completion (all claimants); WITH Notice of Completion = 60 days direct contractor, 30 days subs/suppliers (CC 8412, 8414)
  - Foreclosure: 90 days from recording lien (CC 8460)
  - Stop notices: bonded stop notice freezes construction funds, 30-day release period (CC 8500-8560)
  - CC 8122 (VOID BY LAW): any contract term waiving lien rights is void. Instruct Claude: "If you find pre-payment lien waiver clauses, flag as CRITICAL citing CC 8122"
  - CC 8814 (VOID BY LAW): pay-if-paid clauses conditioning sub payment on owner payment are void. Instruct Claude: "If you find pay-if-paid clauses, flag as CRITICAL citing CC 8814". Also mention retention share: GC must pay sub within 10 days of receiving retention
  - CC 2782 + CC 2782.05 (VOID BY LAW): broad-form indemnity for GC's sole negligence/willful misconduct is void; CC 2782.05 extends to active negligence for contracts after Jan 1 2013. Instruct Claude: "If you find broad-form indemnity clauses, flag as CRITICAL citing CC 2782"
- tokenEstimate must be accurate (count chars/4) and MUST be <= 1500. Target ~1100-1400.

**ca-prevailing-wage.ts** (export name: `caPrevailingWage`):
- id: 'ca-prevailing-wage', domain: 'regulatory', title: 'California Prevailing Wage and DIR Requirements'
- effectiveDate: '2024-01-01', reviewByDate: '2026-07-01'
- Content must include:
  - Public works detection instruction: "First determine if this is a public works contract (government entity as owner, public funding, public building/infrastructure). If NOT public works, skip all prevailing wage findings entirely."
  - Public works thresholds: $25,000+ construction, $15,000+ maintenance
  - DIR registration: required for all public works contractors/subs (LC 1725.5)
  - CPR (Certified Payroll Records): required, penalty $100/calendar day per worker for delinquent records (LC 1776(h))
  - Apprenticeship: required on public works $30,000+ (LC 1777.5)
  - Prevailing wage underpayment penalty: $200/day/worker (LC 1775 -- note in content: "verify current rate, set reviewByDate")
- tokenEstimate <= 1500

**ca-title24.ts** (export name: `caTitle24`):

- effectiveDate: '2023-01-01', reviewByDate: '2025-12-01' (flag 2025 cycle transition)
- Content must include:
  - Current code cycle: 2022 Title 24 Part 6 (effective Jan 1, 2023 through Dec 31, 2025)
  - Prescriptive U-factor: 0.30 max all climate zones, all fenestration

  - Door U-factor (under 25% glazing): 0.20 max
  - NFRC certification required for all fenestration products
  - 2025 cycle effective January 1, 2026 (~30% more restrictive)
  - Instruct Claude: "Flag contracts referencing outdated code cycles (e.g., 2019 Title 24 Part 6)"
- tokenEstimate <= 1500
